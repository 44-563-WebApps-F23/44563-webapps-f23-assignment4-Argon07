Name of the Play: GHOSTS

Author: Henrik Ibsen

MRS. ALVING

--and then I should tell him all I have told you--every word of it.

MANDERS.

You shock me unspeakably, Mrs. Alving.

MRS. ALVING.

Yes; I know that. I know that very well. I myself am shocked at the idea.

[Goes away from the window.]

I am such a coward.
